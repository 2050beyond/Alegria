## Q4 2025 Financial Summary

![Financial Data](https://source.unsplash.com/1200x600/?data,chart,graph,abstract,minimal,blackandwhite)

We are pleased to report strong fourth quarter results, with revenue growth of 23% year-over-year and continued expansion into new markets.

### Revenue Performance

![Revenue Chart](https://source.unsplash.com/1200x600/?chart,graph,line,data,minimal,blackandwhite)

Our revenue reached $47.2 million in Q4, up from $38.4 million in the same period last year. This growth was driven primarily by increased adoption in the enterprise segment and successful expansion into the European market.

### Key Metrics

![Metrics Chart](https://source.unsplash.com/1200x600/?metrics,data,statistics,minimal,blackandwhite)

- **Customer Acquisition**: 340 new enterprise customers
- **Retention Rate**: 94.2% (up from 91.8% in Q3)
- **Average Contract Value**: $142,000 (up 18% YoY)
- **Net Revenue Retention**: 112%

### Geographic Expansion

![Geographic Chart](https://source.unsplash.com/1200x600/?map,world,geography,minimal,blackandwhite)

Our international revenue now represents 34% of total revenue, with particularly strong performance in the UK, Germany, and France. We opened new offices in Amsterdam and Berlin during the quarter.

### Strategic Initiatives

Looking ahead to 2026, we are focused on three key areas:

1. **Product Innovation**: Continued investment in AI-powered features and automation tools
2. **Market Expansion**: Entry into the Asia-Pacific region, beginning with Singapore and Tokyo
3. **Sustainability**: Commitment to carbon neutrality by Q2 2026

### Outlook

We expect Q1 2026 revenue to be in the range of $52-55 million, representing continued strong growth. Our investments in product development and international expansion position us well for the coming year.

We remain committed to building a sustainable, profitable business that creates long-term value for all stakeholders.
